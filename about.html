Who Am I?

Creative Full-Stack Developer

Hello there! My name is Julie, a senior at the University of Washington majoring in Computer Science.

Have you ever encountered problem, and thought, "I wish there was a program that could do this"?

You probably have, and I'm here to create that program.

I am a Full-Stack Developer who is passionate about solving real-world problems using a Computer. With both front-end and back-end developing skills, I design, program, and test applications.

With my skills, I strive to empower people and communities through programming in hopes that we all have the chance to share the small happinesses that we all carry within us.
